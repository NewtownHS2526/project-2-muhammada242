Main Characters

Michael Scofield

A brilliant structural engineer and the protagonist. Michael devises the elaborate escape plan by tattooing blueprints of Fox River Penitentiary on his body. He orchestrates the entire breakout with precision and strategic thinking.

Lincoln Burrows

Michael's brother who is wrongly imprisoned on death row for a crime he didn't commit. Lincoln becomes the focal point of Michael's escape plan and represents the emotional core of the series.

Sarah Tancredi

The prison's senior medical officer who becomes romantically involved with Michael. Sarah is compassionate but conflicted between her duties and her feelings for Michael.

T-Bag Bagwell

A dangerous and unpredictable inmate who becomes part of Michael's team. His ruthlessness and cunning make him both a valuable asset and a threat throughout the series.
