Prison Break

Prison Break is a thrilling television series created by Paul Scheuring. The show follows Michael Scofield, a brilliant structural engineer who devises an elaborate plan to rescue his wrongly imprisoned brother Lincoln Burrows from Fox River Penitentiary.

Main Character

Michael Scofield is the genius engineer at the heart of the escape plan.

The Prison

Fox River Penitentiary becomes the central setting for the elaborate breakout scheme.
