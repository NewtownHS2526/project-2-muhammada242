Plot Summary

The Setup

Michael Scofield, a young structural engineer, learns that his brother Lincoln has been wrongly convicted of murdering the Vice President's brother. With Lincoln on death row, Michael devises an intricate plan to break him out of Fox River Penitentiary by intentionally getting himself imprisoned.

Key Element: The Tattoo Blueprint

The Execution

Inside the prison, Michael carefully builds alliances with other inmates, manipulating situations to move closer to Lincoln. He executes his plan phase by phase, navigating guards, rival inmates, and internal prison politics while keeping his true intentions hidden.

Key Element: Team Formation

The Escape

The brothers execute the escape plan and flee, becoming fugitives. What unfolds is a high-stakes chase across the country as they evade capture, discover a larger conspiracy, and fight for their freedom and justice.

Key Element: Revelation

Did You Know?

The show was created by Paul Scheuring, who drew inspiration from various sources including architecture and true crime stories. The innovative use of tattoos as a plot device became iconic and set the show apart from typical prison dramas.
